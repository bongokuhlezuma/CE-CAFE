Our Story

Your Local Spot in the Northern Suburbs

CE Cafe in Stikland, Bellville, blends rustic warmth with modern comfort. From our leather seating to our wooden accents, every detail is designed to make you feel at home.

Come enjoy freshly brewed coffee, hearty meals, and a welcoming atmosphere — whether you're catching up with friends, working on a laptop, or just taking a break from your day.
